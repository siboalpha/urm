Welcome to URM

URM is a Rwandan based fashion brand that manufactures bags with the help of people living with disabilities. We are dedicated to bringing you exquisite and stylish bags without breaking the bank.

About Us

Our mission is not only to generate income opportunities but to also restore the hope and dreams of people living with disabilities by offering them employment opportunities through our brand. URM is built to provide an opportunity to rebuild livelihoods and re-establish independence of people living with speaking disability by offering them employment opportunities

Our Services

What if there was a chance to make an actual difference? We are a brand that specializes in giving employment opportunities to people living with disabilities. We are able to offer this because of the trusty staff we have working with us. These people have selflessly dedicated themselves to our cause for years, and the best part is that they are so passionate about what we do!

Bag creation

No one likes waste; that's why we created handbags that are made from recycled paper. These bags are strong, sustainable, and gorgeous. Not to mention, they're a fraction of the cost of other handbags.

paper recycling

Say goodbye to paper piles. Recycle papers of all sizes into something new. We can't wait to turn your old magazines, newspapers, old notebooks and more into something useful again.

employment

We work with people with disabilities and their families to help them find employment opportunities that match their skills, interests, and experience.
